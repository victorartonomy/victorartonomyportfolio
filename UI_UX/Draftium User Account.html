Core Values

Make it simple, but significant.

I love deadlines. I like the whooshing sound they make as they fly by.

  • All my works are unique.
  • I Always try to lower the polygon count.
  • Clean UV's
  • I reserve the right to refuse the client for my own reasons - for example, if we do not coincide in what we consider beautiful.
  • Prefer Quality over Quantity
  • As a master, I determine the time that will be needed to complete a 3D. I also assign correction sessions that you definitely need to attend.
